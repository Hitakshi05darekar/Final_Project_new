Matcha Ritual

1. Warm the chawan (tea bowl)

2. Add 2 scoops of matcha

3. Pour 70ml hot water (80°C)

4. Whisk in a W motion

5. Observe the froth form

6. Sip with presence

Stillness in every sip.
